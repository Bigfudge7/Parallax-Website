5 Things You Should Be Doing This Lockdown

Quarantine a time and period of isolation. This lockdown has snatched away our daily life schedules, for our safety ofcourse. But should we stop our lives as well? It doesn't have to be that way. How we spend this time is upon us, let's change our perspective of how we look at this period of time and use this time effectively. Let's use this oppurtunity to be a better version of ourselves.
We'll look at 5 such things that you can implement to do the same.

Plan The Day Ahead

1. Plan The Day Ahead

In my opinion, The Key To A Productive Day Starts the Night Before if you want to have a successful day plan out your whole day the night before which will allow you to spend the upcoming day very efficiently. Set a simple goal which will make you better than the earlier day, this can be cleaning your room or doing cardio for 25 mins instead of 20 mins or covering half of a chapter/topic. Completing a simple goal will make you feel good about yourself and give you a sense of self-accomplishment. But, most importantly you'll have a PURPOSE!

Meditate

2. Meditate / Reflect

Touch your inner space, which is nothingness, as silent and empty as the sky; it is your inner sky. Once you settle down in your inner sky, you have come home, and a great maturity arises in your actions, in your behavior. Then whatever you do has grace in it Meditation can produce a deep state of relaxation and a tranquil mind. During meditation, you focus your attention and eliminate the stream of jumbled thoughts that may be crowding your mind and causing stress.
It's time we be aware of our wandering mind and find inner peace.

exercise

3. Exercise / Eat Healthy

Fitness starts in your head. You must choose to eat clean, exercise regularly, and treat your body with respect. Our bodies are designed to be active, staying at home we can perform many physical activities. Exercising and eating healthy will help us look good, feel good and also boost our immunity. The stronger immune system we have the better prepared we are to fight the virus. Every workout counts even if itâ€™s only for 15 minutes. Just do it. Our goal is not to be perfect but to be better than what we were.
Ofcourse now we can't give the excuse that we don't have time.

Explore a new hobby

4. Explore a new hobby

It is time to start something new and trust the magic of new beginnings. Doing what makes you happy is very important for our mind and body. If you have a hobby you already love then go ahead take it to the next level. Even if you don't have one already it's okay, you finally have enough time to explore new things be it playing an instrument, dancing, playing chess, drawing etc. Make sure you look for your creative side it's just the perfect time to do what makes you happy without having to worry about time being wasted.
Explore new things, get out of that comfort zone!

Communicate

5. Get in touch with your friends,family and colleagues

Use this time to reach out to your friends and family members who you have distanced yourself from due to your regular daily schedules. Spend some quality time with your family and enjoy home cooked food and reduce some of the personal distancing during this social distancing. You can engage in tasks with your family like cooking and cleaning your house etc.
You can also use this time to build contacts online. Connect with people through social media and networking websites like linkedin, get noticed by recruiters, set up new meetings with potential clients and investors, there are endless possibilities in which communicating can help you
